\begin{center}
    \bfseries \zihao{3} 摘~要
\end{center}

针对传统检索增强生成（Retrieval-Augmented Generation, RAG）模型在应对复杂多跳问答任务时面临的线索碎片化与生成端易产生幻觉等问题，本文实现并分析了一套基于置信度重检与后验验证的RAG框架原型。该框架将RAG视为一个兼顾正确率、成本与安全性的序列决策过程，重点围绕两个关键机制展开：基于重排序置信度的伪相关反馈策略（Confidence-based Pseudo-Relevance Feedback），以及基于Chain-of-Verification (CoVe) 的验证约束生成（Verification-constrained Generation）。

首先，在多源数据入库阶段，系统采用序列化对齐策略，将表格与图谱片段转化为统一格式进行检索。其次，在检索决策阶段，系统以重排序得分作为置信度指标，采用启发式阈值控制二次检索的触发，从而在多跳证据覆盖率与计算延迟之间取得平衡。最后，在生成阶段之后，系统执行事实核对验证；当发现生成声明与底层证据存在冲突或知识缺失时，系统将触发安全的“无答案”（No-Answer）拒答机制。

本文共组织了四组实验：系统级全量消融、更严格基线与跨数据集对比、阈值敏感性与部署代价分析，以及错误分布与案例研究。实验结果表明，多源序列化引入的格式噪声导致异构融合模型（Hetero）的有效 F1 分数发生倒退；自适应检索能够部分挽回因证据碎片化导致的损失；而严格的一票否决式CoVe验证导致了56.48\%的灾难性拒答率，使系统可用性断崖式下跌。这一结果说明，本文的主要贡献不在于宣称“全功能系统已经足够鲁棒”，而在于通过可复现实验揭示了RAG系统在效果、成本与可信性之间必须显式权衡的关键矛盾，并据此为后续基于反馈闭环与软置信度的多跳问答系统优化提供了明确方向。

\begin{center}
    \bfseries \zihao{3} Abstract
\end{center}

To address the challenges of evidence fragmentation and generation hallucination in traditional Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) models, this thesis implements and analyzes a robust RAG framework based on confidence-aware re-retrieval and post-hoc verification. By formulating RAG as a sequential decision process that balances accuracy, cost, and safety, the framework focuses on two core mechanisms: confidence-based Pseudo-Relevance Feedback (PRF) and Verification-constrained Generation based on Chain-of-Verification (CoVe).

First, in the data ingestion phase, the system employs a serialization alignment strategy to unify tables and knowledge graph snippets for retrieval. Second, in the retrieval decision phase, the system uses reranking scores as a confidence metric and adopts a heuristic threshold to trigger secondary retrieval, thereby striking a balance between multi-hop evidence coverage and computational latency. Finally, in the generation phase, the system performs fact-checking verification; when conflicts or knowledge gaps are detected between the generated claims and the underlying evidence, the system triggers a safe "No-Answer" rejection mechanism. 

Comprehensive full-scale experiments reveal that format noise introduced by multi-source serialization causes a regression in the F1 score of the heterogeneous fusion model (Hetero). While adaptive retrieval partially mitigates losses due to evidence fragmentation, the strict veto-style CoVe verification leads to a catastrophic 56.48\% rejection rate, causing a precipitous drop in system usability. This counterintuitive finding highlights the risks of naive module stacking in RAG systems and underscores the fundamental tension between effectiveness, cost, and reliability that must be explicitly balanced. By diagnosing these system-level vulnerabilities, this thesis provides clear directions for future optimizations of multi-hop QA systems based on feedback loops and soft confidence estimation.
